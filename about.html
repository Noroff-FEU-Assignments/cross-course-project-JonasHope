Our story

Jackets for pioneers through a century of adventure.

Rainydays has been run by dedicated and passionate outdoor enthusiasts for over 50 years. It all started with the famous hiker Will Smith, an avid hunter and outdoorsman. He introduced the well known iconic series. An invention that among others Brad Pit recognized and made use of on his expeditions. No other supplier of jackets can compare with our outdoor and rainy history, or our efforts to share the experiences with today's outdoor people. Rainydays has for over 50 years played a crucial role in the Norwegian outdoor life.
